1 & 2 Materials in Mechanical Engineering Materials in Mechanical Engineering" is a foundational topic that explores the various materials used in the design and manufacturing of mechanical systems. This includes understanding the properties, applications, and selection criteria for metals, polymers, ceramics, and composites. A thorough knowledge of these materials helps engineers to choose the right material for specific applications, ensuring efficiency, durability, and cost-effectiveness in mechanical designs. Materials Overview
Mechanical Properties 		Watch Video 1 Watch Video 2
3 & 4 Mechanical Measurements and Drawing Mechanical Measurements and Drawing" is a critical area in mechanical engineering that focuses on the accurate measurement of physical dimensions and the creation of technical drawings. These skills are essential for designing, manufacturing, and inspecting mechanical components and systems. Mechanical measurements ensure that components meet specified tolerances, while technical drawings provide detailed visual representations of components, including dimensions, tolerances, and material specifications.
